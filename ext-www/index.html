Documenting Scientific Workflows

The Metadata, Provenance, and Ontology Project

The MPO ProjectAutomated Metadata, Provenance Catalog, And Navigable Interfaces: Ensuring The Usefulness Of Extreme-Scale Data is an ongoing multi-institutional effort aimed at developing tools to automatically document scientific workflows and all associated information, allowing traceability in either direction – answering the questions: “Where was this data used?” or “Where did the data in this figure come from.”.
